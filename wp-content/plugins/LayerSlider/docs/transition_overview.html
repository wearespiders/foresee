About LayerSlider WP Transition Builder

Since version 4.0.0, LayerSlider WP suppors various types of layer transitions. The Transition Builder offers you an easy-to-use visual editor to create your own transitions for your own taste.

Transitions created with this editor will automatically appear in list of the available transition of the transition gallery where you can choose and use them in your slider.
